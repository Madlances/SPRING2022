Super Smash Bros League

About us

Super Smash Bros League is a competive gameplay where teams can go against each other and try to win the Golden Cup.
To do this they will need to fight each other head on.

Rules

  • Each team needs to to launch their opponents off the stage and out of bounds.
  • To knock out an opposing player, the player must knock that player outside the stage's boundaries to the right or left.
  • When a player is pushed or hit off the stage, the player can attempt to "recover" by jumping or use any other object they brought to grab on to the stage.
  • Additionally, some players vary in weight, with lighter palyers being easier to get off the stage than heavy characters.
